Every Meridian agent deployed on Avalanche is a persistent gas consumer, contributing to AVAX burn through its continuous decision cycle. Meridian is DeFi-first, not DeFi-adjacent. ElizaOS targets social agents, GOAT offers stateless blockchain actions, Virtuals operates as a token launchpad.
